About me

Hello! I'm Stefanie Holbrook, a frontend developer living in Salt Lake City, Utah

I began my journey in frontend development on the road less traveled. In 2011 I began learning HTML and CSS for a project I was working on, and I have since become very passionate about development and have worked on projects using AngularJS, jQuery, CasperJS, Ruby on Rails, Node.js.

I am always looking for new projects to further grow my experience and knowledge. I pride myself on my ability to problem solve, adapt quickly to any work environment, and take on a variety of projects with ease.
